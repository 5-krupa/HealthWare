Made From CaneFiber®

Canefiber® or Sugarcane Bagasse is a fibrous by-product of sugar industry. The husk which is left after the juice has been extracted from the sugarcane. It’s intrinsic properties make it a great raw material for making tableware products such as bowls, plates, takeaway containers. Bagasse is also a perfect replacement for plastics & paper in retail packaging.

NO PLASTIC IS FANTASTIC

Say No To Plastics

  • Plastic is not biodegradable and does not go away; It will take hundreds of years for a single piece of plastic to decompose, and then it will turn into a dangerous microplastic.
  • Avoiding plastic is a better solution than recycling as the recycling process also is carbon intensive.

Government of India has banned the manufacture, sale and use of identified single-use plastic items like plates, cups, straws, trays, and polystyrene from Jul 1, 2022. Read the gazette notification here.
